# 10 Interview Questions to Ask Your Job Interviewer

![Interview Questions](interview-questions.jpeg)
*Source: /Users/tvl/Documents/repos/tvl/career/career-tvl/za/cloudsmiths/interview-questions.jpeg*

---

## 1. What does success look like in this role after 6 months?

- If they can't answer clearly, they don't know what they want from you.

---

## 2. Why is this position open?

- Is someone leaving right now?
- Did someone get promoted or leave?
- You need to know which it is – changes the dynamic.

---

## 3. What's the biggest challenge the team is facing right now?

- Shows if they're thinking about their future.

---

## 4. How will you measure my performance?

- Not just "hit your goals" – you want to know *how*.

---

## 5. Can you tell me about the team I'll work with?

- Your coworkers matter.
- How do they collaborate? Do they support each other well?

---

## 6. What's your management style and how do you help your team grow?

- Bad managers talk in circles, good ones give you real examples.

---

## 7. How does the company invest in employee development?

- If they stumble here, run.
- Learning and growth, or stagnation?

---

## 8. What do the people who thrive here have in common?

- This tells you if you fit.

---

## 9. What's changed at the company in the past year?

- Easy to answer – shows they're on top of things.

---

## 10. What concerns do you have about my fit for this role?

- Bold question that shows confidence and lets you clear up any doubts.
